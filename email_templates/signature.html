Step 1 — Paste the signature into Gmail

  1. Click Copy Signature for Gmail above.
  2. Open Gmail → gear icon ⚙ → See all settings → General tab.
  3. Scroll to Signature → click Create new → name it Infinity Faith.
  4. Click inside the signature text box and press ⌘V (Mac) / Ctrl+V (Windows).
    The full signature appears — name, tagline, buttons, and the clickable banner.
  5. Scroll to the bottom of Settings and click Save Changes.

Step 2 — Confirm the banner is clickable

  1. Compose a test email to yourself and send it.
  2. Open the received email and click the banner — it should open https://www.infinity-faith.com.
  3. The banner image is hosted at infinity-faith.com/images/email-banner.png — no manual upload needed.

Alternatively — host the banner at a URL

  • Your website — Upload via your CMS and paste the direct image URL into src="banner.png" in this file.
  • Google Drive — Upload → Share (Anyone with link) → use:
    https://drive.google.com/uc?export=view&id=YOUR_FILE_ID
